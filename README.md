# Resunora

**Tailor every application. Land more interviews.**

Resunora is a web app that helps job seekers optimize their resume for each role they apply to. Instead of sending the same document everywhere, you upload your resume, paste the job posting, and get a clear picture of how well you match � plus specific steps to improve.

---

## About

Applying with a generic resume makes it hard to stand out, especially when companies use applicant tracking systems (ATS) to filter candidates before a recruiter ever reads your file.

Resunora analyzes your resume against a job description and shows you where you align, where you fall short, and what to change. The goal is simple: give you targeted, actionable feedback so every application is stronger than the last.

---

## Features

- **Match score** � See an overall fit percentage and category breakdowns for skills, experience, keywords, and education.
- **Keyword matching** � Compare your resume to the job posting and spot missing skills and terms.
- **Strengths & gaps** � Understand what you already bring to the role and what needs work.
- **Actionable suggestions** � Get section-level improvements with clear, specific fixes � not vague advice.
- **ATS optimization** � Feedback geared toward passing automated screening and reaching a human reviewer.
- **Multi-format upload** � Upload PDF, Word, or image files of your resume.
- **Instant results** � Full analysis in seconds, with a live progress view while your report is generated.
- **User accounts** � Sign up, log in, and access your optimizer dashboard anytime.

---

## How it works

1. **Upload your resume** � Drop in a PDF, Word doc, or image.
2. **Paste the job description** � Include requirements, responsibilities, and preferred skills.
3. **Review your report** � Match score, missing keywords, strengths, and improvement tips � ready to act on.
